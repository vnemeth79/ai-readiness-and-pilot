# AI Pilot Project Assessment Framework

## Assessment Dimensions

### 1. Business Pain Points & Opportunities
- Customer experience challenges
- Operational inefficiencies
- Data management issues
- Revenue optimization opportunities
- Cost reduction potential

### 2. Data Readiness
- Data availability and accessibility
- Data quality and structure
- Data governance maturity
- Integration capabilities

### 3. Organizational Readiness
- Leadership support for AI initiatives
- Technical talent availability
- Change management capacity
- Budget allocation readiness

### 4. Process Maturity
- Current automation level
- Process documentation
- Standardization across operations
- Measurement and analytics capability

### 5. Strategic Priorities
- Time-to-value urgency
- Competitive pressure
- Regulatory compliance needs
- Innovation vs. optimization focus

### 6. AI Strategic Intent
- Primary motivation for AI exploration
- Scope of AI ambition (experiment vs. tool vs. transformation)
- Alignment between readiness and ambition
- Appropriate project sizing and expectations

## Question Structure (14 Questions)

### Section A: Business Context (3 questions)
1. Industry vertical and primary business model
2. Top 3 business challenges currently facing
3. Primary strategic goal for next 12 months

### Section B: Data & Technology (3 questions)
4. Current data infrastructure maturity
5. Existing automation/AI tools in use
6. Data accessibility and quality assessment

### Section C: Organizational Capability (4 questions)
7. Technical team size and AI expertise
8. Leadership alignment on AI initiatives
9. Budget readiness for pilot projects
10. Self-assessed AI readiness level (Beginner/Intermediate/Advanced/Expert)

### Section D: Strategy & Priorities (4 questions)
11. Primary AI strategic intent (Experimentation & Learning / Tool Adoption / Strategic Transformation)
12. Most time-consuming manual processes
13. Areas with highest error rates or inefficiencies
14. Desired timeline for seeing ROI from AI pilots

## AI Solution Categories to Map

1. **Customer Intelligence & Personalization**
   - Chatbots and virtual assistants
   - Recommendation engines
   - Sentiment analysis
   - Customer segmentation

2. **Process Automation**
   - Document processing (OCR, extraction)
   - Workflow automation
   - Intelligent routing
   - RPA enhancement with AI

3. **Predictive Analytics**
   - Demand forecasting
   - Churn prediction
   - Maintenance prediction
   - Risk assessment

4. **Content & Knowledge Management**
   - Content generation
   - Knowledge base automation
   - Document summarization
   - Search optimization

5. **Quality & Compliance**
   - Anomaly detection
   - Quality control automation
   - Compliance monitoring
   - Fraud detection

6. **Sales & Marketing Optimization**
   - Lead scoring
   - Campaign optimization
   - Pricing optimization
   - Sales forecasting
